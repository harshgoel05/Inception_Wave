About us

IWSC Drc Chapter focuses on building work culture and flourishing innovation among drc students by providing a common platform to connect students of various departments having common interests,so as to help them to upgrade their skills. It is an interactive network where students get to know more about their college,college students and their stories,alumni,culture and traditions of the college.
